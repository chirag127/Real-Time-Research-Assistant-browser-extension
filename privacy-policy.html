Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Real-Time Research Assistant browser extension handles your data. We value your privacy and are committed to transparency.

Required Permissions

  • storage: Save your preferences and settings locally.
  • activeTab: Interact with the currently active webpage to detect topics.
  • tabs: Detect active pages and provide relevant suggestions.
  • host permissions: Access specific domains to analyze content and suggest articles.
